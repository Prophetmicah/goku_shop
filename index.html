Introduction

Goku, the legendary Saiyan warrior from the Dragon Ball series, is undoubtedly one of the coolest and most iconic characters in anime history. His unwavering spirit, incredible strength, and determination to protect his loved ones and the universe have captured the hearts of fans worldwide.

Powers

  • The ability to fly and manipulate energy blasts (Ki blasts)
  • Mastery of various martial arts techniques
  • Rapid healing and recovery
